About this tracker

This is an independent, public-interest tracker of avian influenza ("bird flu") — all strains, including H5N1, H7 and H9. It brings official detections from several agencies into one map and list so anyone can see where the virus is being found — in poultry, wild birds, wild mammals, dairy cattle, and people. Use the Strain filter to focus on a specific subtype.

Data sources

    Not medical advice. Figures are aggregated from public reports and may lag or be revised by the source agencies. Current human health risk to the general public is assessed by public-health authorities. For guidance, consult the CDC, WHO, or your national/state health department. If you keep birds or work with livestock, report sick or dead animals to your local agriculture authority.
